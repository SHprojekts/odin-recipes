Carbonara!

Ingredienti:

  • 4 Egg Yolks
  • 200g Guanciale or Pancetta
  • 30g Grana Padano or Parmessan Romano
  • 20g Pecorino Romano
  • Pepper to taste

Method:

  1. Pepper eggs, add cheese to eggs and whisk eggs together until everything is mixed
  2. Cook guanciale or pancetta until done and crispy. Set aside meat and add oil to egg mixture and mix
  3. Cook pasta and add a ladle of finished cooking liquid to egg mixture and mix until combined
  4. Add pasta to egg mixture and finish cooking over bain-marie to 149 degrees
  5. Add room temperature guanciale or pancetta to pasta with a ladle of cooking liquid and cook until warm throughout
